About Our Campus - 42 Barcelona

42 Barcelona is the most innovative and free programming campus that opens the doors to the job market. With a revolutionary methodology and an average duration of three years, it is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week so you can learn at your own pace.

To be part of 42 Barcelona you don't need any previous knowledge or qualification of any kind, just be over 18 years old, eager to learn, attitude and perseverance. You only need to pass two phases: an online registration test and a 26-day face-to-face selection period.
